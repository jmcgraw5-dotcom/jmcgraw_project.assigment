Welcome to Phoenix Bikers Club

Phoenix Bikers Club is a passionate community of street and sports motorcycle riders dedicated to creating an open and fun atmosphere for all. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or new to the scene, we welcome you to join us as we explore the open road and build lasting friendships.

Who We Are

We are street and sports motorcycle enthusiasts brought together by our love of riding and adventure. Our goal is simple: to foster a welcoming community where riders can share experiences, support each other, and have an unforgettable time on and off the road.

Our Home Away From Home

You'll often find us hanging out at the Dutch parking lot, where we spend countless hours together as a crew. It's not just a parking lot to us—it's where the magic happens. We gather for rides, catch up with friends, and sometimes even break out into impromptu karaoke sessions that keep the energy and spirits high!

Community & Memories

From holiday celebrations to workshop sessions, Phoenix Bikers Club is all about creating unforgettable memories together. We're more than just a motorcycle club—we're a family united by our passion for riding and brotherhood.
